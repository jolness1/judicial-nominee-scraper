On February 12, 2018 President Trump nominated Mark J. Bennett to fill a seat on the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit. Bennett was nominated to replace Judge Richard R. Clifton, who assumed senior status on December 31, 2016.

The Senate Judiciary Committee should carefully examine Mark Bennett’s record before putting him in a lifetime seat on the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit.
