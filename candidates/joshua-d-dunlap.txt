Biography

Joshua D. Dunlap graduated from Notre Dame Law School in 2008 and Pensacola Christian College in 2005.


Legal Experience

Dunlap works at Pierce Atwood LLP (2009 – Present). He is currently a partner in the litigation group (Jan. 2018 – Present) after previously serving as a litigation associate (Sept. 2009 – Dec. 2017). He was a judicial law clerk, for Judge Paul Kelly Jr. at the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Tenth Circuit (Sept. 2008 – Sept. 2009) and a summer associate (May 2007 – Aug. 2007).
