President Joe Biden nominated Eunice Lee to the United States Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit on May 12, 2021 . She has spent her career as a public defender in New York City. When confirmed on August 7, 2021, Judge Lee became the only judge on the circuit with experience as a public defender and only the second Black woman to ever serve on the Second Circuit.
