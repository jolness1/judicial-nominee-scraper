On March 11, 2019, President Trump nominated Jeffrey V. Brown to the United States District Court for the Southern District of Texas for the seat previously held by Judge Melinda Harmon . Brown is currently a justice on the Supreme Court of Texas, a position he has held since 2013. He previously acted as a state court judge for the Texas Fourteenth Court of Appeals and 55th District Court. He has been a member of the conservative Federalist Society since 1997.
