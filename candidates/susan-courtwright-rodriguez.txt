Biography

Susan Courtwright Rodriguez received her J.D from the Antonin Scalia Law School, George Mason University (2005 – 2009) and her B.A. from Centre College (1999 – 2003) .


Legal Experience

Rodriguez is currently a U.S. Magistrate Judge, U.S. District Court for the Western District of North Carolina (April 2023 – Present).
