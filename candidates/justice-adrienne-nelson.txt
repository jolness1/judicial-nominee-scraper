On July 14, 2022, President Biden nominated Justice Adrienne Nelson to the United States District Court for the District of Oregon to the seat vacated by Judge Michael Mosman. Justice Nelson previously served on the Supreme Court of the State of Oregon and has extensive experience in public defense and on the bench. When confirmed on February 15, 2023, she became the first Black woman to serve on the United States District Court for the District of Oregon.
