Biography

William Walter “Bill” Mercer received his J.D. from George Mason University School of Law (1989 – 1993), his M.P.A. from Harvard University (1986 – 1988), and his B.A. from the University of Montana (1982 – 1986).


Legal Experience

Mercer is currently a state representative in the Montana House of Representatives (Jan. 2019 – Present), after being elected in November 2018,  and then re-elected in Nov. 2020, Nov. 2022, and Nov. 2024.  He works at Holland & Hart LLP (2010 – Present) — as a partner since 2014 and of counsel from 2010-2013.

Mercer has served in the U.S. Attorney’s Office District of Montana (2001 – 2009) and the U.S. Department of Justice (2005 – 2007), and was Assistant U.S.  Attorney, U.S. Attorney’s Office District of Montana (1997 – 2001).
