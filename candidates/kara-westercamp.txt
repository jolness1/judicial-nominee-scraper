On February 12, 2026, President Trump nominated Kara Westercamp to the United States Court of International Trade.


Biography

Westercamp comes from Cedar Rapids, Iowa. She received a B.A. in Political Science and a B.B.A. in Finance and Management from the University of Iowa in 2005. In 2009, she graduated with an MBA from the University of Iowa College of Business, as well as a J.D. from the University of Iowa College of Law.
