On January 19, 2022, President Joe Biden nominated Nusrat Choudhury to the United States District Court for the Eastern District of New York to the seat vacated by Judge Josephy Bianco. A dedicated civil rights and civil liberties attorney, Ms. Choudhury has spent her career advocating for equal justice under the law. When confirmed on June 15, 2023, Ms. Choudhury became the first Muslim woman and the first Bangladeshi American to serve as a federal judge.
