On August 20, 2025, President Trump nominated Lindsey Ann Freeman to the U.S. District Court for the Middle District of North Carolina. She was confirmed on December 2, 2025.


Biography

Lindsey Ann Freeman earned degrees from University of Pennsylvania Law School (2008 – 2011), Wharton Business School; Certificate in Business and Public Policy (2009 – 2011), and Harvard University (2001 – 2005).


Legal Experience

Freeman currently works at the U.S. Attorney’s Office for the Middle District of North Carolina as First Assistant U.S. Attorney (2025 – Present) and Assistant U.S. Attorney (2021 – present).  She has also worked as Chief of Staff and Deputy Associate Attorney General at the U.S. Department of Justice Office of the Associate Attorney General (2020).
