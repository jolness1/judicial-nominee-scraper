On August 12, 2025, Donald Trump nominated Edmund G. LaCour to the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of Alabama. He was confirmed on October 29, 2025.

View/download the fact sheet here . View/download our letter of opposition here .


Biography

Edmund G. LaCour received a Bachelor of Arts from Birmingham-Southern College in 2007, a Master of Philosophy from Trinity College Dublin in 2008, and a Juris Doctor from Yale Law School in 2011.

This is the second time Trump has nominated LaCour for a judicial seat. In June 2020, he nominated him to the District Court for the Middle District of Alabama.
